Deck Nine Games:

I fiercely desire to assist in the production of high-quality narrative adventure games with meaningful story and rich gameplay at Deck Nine Games. I have always loved single player games with enthralling atmosphere and enriched emotion provoking tales. Developers and publishers like Deck Nine and Square Enix have greatly influenced me since childhood with their lovable yet believably flawed characters and sensationally captivating stories. My passion, teamwork, and problem-solving ability will make me an asset to the team. Deck Nine’s philosophy and game style resonate strongly with my passions and goals.

I am currently a computer science student with a passion for adaptable, clean, independent programming. Video games are connected to the most meaningful experiences and nostalgia in my life and a shared passion of my friends and family. I feel games are lacking connection and want to give those kinds of experiences to others which has inspired me to pursue innovation and development of games.

While I am independently motivated, I appreciate collective efforts and collaborate productively within group settings. In over 12 years in the Marine Corps I have professionally been in teams both large and intimately small made of people from all walks of life, diverse backgrounds, and upbringing.

In my former role managing communication systems I exercised a detailed, calculated and methodical approach to problem solving in order to isolate and repair system faults. Moreover, I am competent in C# in video game engines and animation proficiency in graphics editors for 3D asset creation.

My current knowledge and experience coupled with my cohesive enthusiasm and critical solution finding cultivated in the Marines would make me an ideal candidate for a position in a highly passionate team like Deck Nine. I look forward to the opportunity to continue serving others, creating amazing content, and working with my community.

Sincerely,

John-David A. Amador
